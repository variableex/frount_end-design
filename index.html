NextDoor Global Services ( NDGS ) is an online marketing and web design company founded by Mr. Archit Bhateja in 2014. It has registered offices in the US, India and Germany. During a short span of time it has acquired a clientele of hundreds of satisfied customers. Ms. Manpreet joined the company as director in July 2019 and started working on various projects of the Ministry of Education of Germany and German embassy, New Delhi. Mr. Archit and Ms. Manpreet also participated in the World Mentor competition organised by the Ministry of education of Germany in October 2019 and won the first prize. In March 2020 they received the authorisation to organise the entrance exam for students wishing to pursue higher education in Germany and provide them with all the necessary information and consultation. In April 2020, Mr. Archit and Ms. Manpreet successfully registered NDGS in Germany as a start up and started a new chapter in the journey of NDGS. In May 2020, NDGS received the prestigious “ Start Up of the year 2020-2021 “ award conferred by the Ministry of Education, Science and Culture of Germany. Finally as recently as 6th June 2020, the Ministry of Education, Science and Culture of Germany chose NDGS for its new and exciting project of developing education portals for its affiliated schools spread over 190 countries. Students of such affiliated schools will be able to see their semester classes, time tables, courses, etc. on these portals and also download the learning materials in PDF form. Teachers will be able to login in these portals as well and during examination period, students can directly register for their examinations on the respective portal.
